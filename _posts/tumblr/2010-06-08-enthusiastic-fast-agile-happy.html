Enthusiastic, fast, agile, happy

— Support at 37signals: Why it’s awesome - (37signals)

Fast is important for good customer service, I knew that already about 37signals’ way of doing things.

Those four things are hard to introduce in a work culture all at once:

  • Enthusiastic but not Fast?
  • Fast, but not enthusiastic?
  • Fast, but not Agile (relunctant)?
  • Fast, but not Happy?
  • Agile, but not Happy?

Those are four tough values to adopt.

Taking a page from zappos, I’m betting the value that makes the others happen is, simply, happiness.
