This blog is about what it means to aim for two things at once: leading a modern life, with all its nuances and its adjustments, and seeking to live out the Gospel, the challenging and beautiful way that Jesus Christ proposes.

Maybe you're into to the Gospel but you have trouble being in touch with the modern world.

Maybe you recognize that modernity has better answers and yet you're intrigued by modern Christian models who live out their faith with integrity.

Maybe you've been living both of these for a while and you'd like to connect with others that live the same way.

If so, you might be interested in the articles from this blog. I publish an article every few months.
